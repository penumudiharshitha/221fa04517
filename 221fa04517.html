Projects

Adult height prediction

Adult height prediction uses statistical and machine learning models to estimate an individual's final height based on various factors, such as parental heights and growth patterns. By analyzing historical data and incorporating genetic and environmental variables, these models provide a probabilistic estimate of future height. This approach helps in understanding growth trends and making informed predictions about potential height outcomes.

Water quality prediction

Water quality prediction involves using machine learning models to forecast the quality of water based on various parameters. By analyzing historical data on factors like temperature, pH, turbidity, and pollutant levels, these models can predict future water conditions. This helps in identifying potential contamination events and ensuring safe drinking water. Accurate predictions are essential for effective water management and public health protection.
